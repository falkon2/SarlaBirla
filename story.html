Our Story

In contrast to other pickle brands, Old Town Pickle was made with explicit expectation to appear as something else. We realize numerous individuals love tangy and spicy combination yet a large number of them loathe or are unconscious of the regularly unfortunate fixings that make a pickle taste so great.

Old Town Pickle has develped to incorporate four superb takeout areas in Toronto with additional to come sooner rather than later.
